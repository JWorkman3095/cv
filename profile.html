Life Before Bootcamp

Prior to attending MAX Technical Training Bootcamp I spent years working in retail warehousing, maunfacturing and the logistics industry. I am also a U.S. Army Veteran and spent my fomative years, after high school, training throughout the United States and Europe. When the pandemic hit in 2020 I found myself unemployed and wondering what I was going to do next. I knew that years of being a singer-songwriter and touring to suppliment my income was also not going to provide the lifestyle I had grown acustom to, especially since that industry was also hit hard. I spoke with a now former band mate, who graduated in 2021, and he began telling me all about the great experience he had. When everyone returned back to work I found myself working in a location I disiked GREATLY. I had never been more unhappy getting up everyday to go to a job than I was in 2021. That is when I made the decision to alter my course and take matters into my own hands. I called MAX!
