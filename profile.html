Life Before Bootcamp

Prior to attending MAX Technical Training Bootcamp I spent years working in retail warehousing, maunfacturing and the logistics industry. When the pandemic hit in 2020 I found myself unemployed and wondering what I was going to do next. I knew that years of being a singer-songwriter and touring to suppliment my income was also not going to provide the lifestyle I had grown acustom to, especially since that industry was also hit hard. I spoke with a now former band mate, who graduated in 2021, and he told me all about the great experience he had during his enrollment. When everyone started returning back to work I found myself in an environment that wasn't going to last long or provide me with any oppurtunities for advancment. I had never been more unhappy getting up everyday to go to a job than I was in 2021. That is when I made the decision to alter my course and take matters into my own hands. I called MAX!
